Welcome to Marvel Rivals Coaching

Here, you'll find resources to improve your gameplay, learn team compositions, and hone your skills as a Marvel Rivals player.

When people come to the site they can usually expect to find a personalized experience on what they need to do to get better at the game. That can be through learning different tactics and play styles that they need to be implementing or learning how to work better with their teams.

Welcome to our tier list of the best heroes in Marvel Rivals! This list is based on the game’s Season 1.5 mid-season patch that launched on 3/14/25. We will continue to update as the meta develops and balance changes are added. As always with our tier lists, we recommend starting with whatever heroes you feel best suit your personal preference, playstyle and strengths. Without further ado, here are our rankings for the best heroes in Marvel Rivals. Use the Table of Contents to jump to the explanation for any individual hero or tier.
