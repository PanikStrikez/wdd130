Content

what is the purpose of this page?

For this website I want to mainly focus on the game called Marvel rivals and in that game I'm a coach for a team so I would like to create a website where me and my players can go and put together different team compositions different strategies and how to attack and defend and what my individual players statistics are.

Images for the Home page

what can you get out of this page?

When people come to the site they can usually expect to find a personalized experience on what they need to do to get better at the game. That can be through learning different tactics and play styles that they need to be implementing or learning how to work better with their teams.
